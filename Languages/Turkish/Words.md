Rüşvet - this is a Turkish word meaning bribe. It refers to offering or receiving something of value to influence the actions of an individual in a position of power, often in an unethical or illegal way.

Haraç - this Turkish term means tribute or extortion. Historically, it referred to a tax or levy imposed, often by a ruling authority. In modern usage, it can also mean a forced payment, akin to extortion.
