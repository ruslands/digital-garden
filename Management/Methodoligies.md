
### **1. ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library)**

* **What it is**: A widely used framework for **IT service management (ITSM)**.
* **Purpose**: Helps organizations deliver IT services efficiently and aligned with business needs.
* **Key Concepts**: Service lifecycle, incident management, change management, continual service improvement.

---

### **2. IIBA (International Institute of Business Analysis)**

* **What it is**: A **professional association** for business analysts.
* **Known for**: Creating and maintaining the **BABOK Guide**.

#### **BABOK (Business Analysis Body of Knowledge)**

* A **methodology and framework** describing standard practices for **business analysis**, including:

  * Elicitation
  * Requirements analysis
  * Solution assessment
  * Stakeholder engagement

---

### **3. IREB (International Requirements Engineering Board)**

* **What it is**: A non-profit organization that offers certification in **Requirements Engineering**.
* **Known for**: The **CPRE (Certified Professional for Requirements Engineering)** certification.
* **Focus**: Standardizing and improving how requirements are gathered, documented, and managed in software and system development.

---

### **4. IQBBA (International Qualification Board for Business Analysis)**

* **What it is**: A global organization that offers **certification in business analysis**.
* **Key Certification**: **Certified Foundation Level Business Analyst (CFLBA)**.
* **Focus**: Business process improvement, requirements engineering, and solution evaluation.

---

### **5. IPMA (International Project Management Association)**

* **What it is**: A federation of over 70 national project management associations.
* **Certifications**: IPMA Level A (most advanced) to D (entry level).
* **Focus**: Competency-based project management, including behavioral and contextual skills, not just technical.

---

### **6. PMI (Project Management Institute)**

* **What it is**: A leading **global organization for project management**.
* **Known for**: The **PMP (Project Management Professional)** certification and **PMBOK Guide**.
* **Focus**: Standardizing project management processes and promoting best practices globally.

---

### **7. CAP (Certified Analytics Professional)**

* **What it is**: A **vendor-neutral analytics certification** managed by INFORMS.
* **Focus**: End-to-end analytics process, including:

  * Problem framing
  * Data analysis
  * Modeling
  * Deployment
  * Model lifecycle management
* **Ideal for**: Data scientists, analysts, and professionals who apply analytics to business decisions.
