## Introduction

**МТК (Малые Технологические Компании)** - Small Technology Companies registry in Russia.

**Purpose:** Support and development of technology companies through tax benefits, grants, and other forms of government support.

## МТК Status Requirements

### Status Duration

**МТК Status:** Given for three years with possibility of extension.

---

### Monitoring Requirements

**ОКВЭД (OKVED) Monitoring:**
- ОКВЭД codes are monitored
- **Important:** Cannot change ОКВЭД codes, otherwise will be excluded from МТК registry

**Purpose:** Ensure company remains in technology sector

---

### Intellectual Property Requirements

**For Inclusion in МТК Registry:**
- Requires presence of **РИД (Result of Intellectual Activity)** or application for РИД

**Accepted Types of РИД:**
1. **Топология микросхемы** (Integrated Circuit Topology)
2. **Полезная модель** (Utility Model)
3. **ЭВМ** (Computer Program / Software)

**Purpose:** Demonstrate technological innovation and intellectual property

## Key Requirements Checklist

✅ **For МТК Status:**
- [ ] Have РИД (Result of Intellectual Activity) or application for РИД
- [ ] РИД type must be: Integrated Circuit Topology, Utility Model, or Software
- [ ] Appropriate ОКВЭД codes (technology sector)
- [ ] Cannot change ОКВЭД codes after registration

✅ **Maintenance:**
- [ ] Status valid for 3 years
- [ ] Can be extended
- [ ] ОКВЭД codes are monitored
- [ ] Changing ОКВЭД will result in exclusion from registry

## Benefits of МТК Status

**Potential Benefits:**
- Tax benefits
- Grant support
- Participation in government programs
- Access to expert centers
- Visibility on startup showcase platform

---

## Best Practices

1. **Prepare РИД early** - Have intellectual property ready before applying
2. **Choose ОКВЭД carefully** - Cannot be changed after registration
3. **Maintain technology focus** - Stay within technology sector
4. **Use showcase platform** - Leverage Vzlet for visibility
5. **Follow regulations** - Stay compliant with monitoring requirements
