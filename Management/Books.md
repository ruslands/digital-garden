## Technical Books

### Software Development

**Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship** by Robert C. Martin
- **Focus:** Writing clean, maintainable code
- **Key Topics:** Code quality, refactoring, best practices

**Designing Data-Intensive Applications** by Martin Kleppmann
- **Focus:** Building scalable data systems
- **Key Topics:** Data storage, processing, distributed systems

**Patterns of Enterprise Application Architecture** by Martin Fowler
- **Focus:** Enterprise application design patterns
- **Key Topics:** Architecture patterns, design principles

**Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software** by Erich Gamma
- **Also Known As:** "Gang of Four" book
- **Focus:** Object-oriented design patterns
- **Key Topics:** Creational, structural, behavioral patterns

**The Pragmatic Programmer: From Journeyman to Master** by David Thomas
- **Focus:** Practical programming wisdom
- **Key Topics:** Best practices, career development

---

### Algorithms and Data Structures

**Искусство Программирования** by Donald Knuth
- **English:** The Art of Computer Programming
- **Focus:** Comprehensive computer science reference
- **Key Topics:** Algorithms, data structures, mathematical foundations

**Алгоритмы и структуры данных** by Niklaus Wirth
- **English:** Algorithms and Data Structures
- **Focus:** Fundamental algorithms and data structures

**Математические методы анализа алгоритмов** by Donald Knuth and Ronald Graham
- **English:** Mathematical Methods for Algorithm Analysis
- **Focus:** Mathematical foundations of algorithm analysis

**Алгоритмы. Построение и анализ** by Thomas H. Cormen
- **English:** Introduction to Algorithms
- **Focus:** Comprehensive algorithm textbook

**Грокаем Алгоритмы** by Aditya Bhargava
- **English:** Grokking Algorithms
- **Focus:** Visual, beginner-friendly algorithm learning

**Aho, Hopcroft, Ullman "Design And Analysis Of Computer Algorithms"**
- **Focus:** Algorithm design and analysis

**«Банда четырех»: Приемы объектно-ориентированного проектирования. Паттерны проектирования.**
- **English:** "Gang of Four" - Design Patterns
- **Focus:** Object-oriented design patterns

---

### Systems and Networks

**Современные Операционные Системы** by Andrew Tanenbaum
- **English:** Modern Operating Systems
- **Focus:** Operating system concepts and design

**Компиляторы. Принципы, технологии, инструменты** by Alfred Aho, Ravi Sethi, Jeffrey Ullman
- **English:** Compilers: Principles, Techniques, and Tools
- **Focus:** Compiler design and implementation

**Олиферов «Компьютерные сети»**
- **English:** Computer Networks
- **Focus:** Network protocols and architecture

**Таненбаума «Компьютерные сети»**
- **English:** Computer Networks by Tanenbaum
- **Focus:** Network fundamentals and protocols

---

### Databases and Transactions

**Transactional Information Systems: Theory, Algorithms, and the Practice of Concurrency Control and Recovery** by Weikum and Vossen
- **Focus:** Database transaction management
- **Key Topics:** Concurrency control, recovery, ACID properties

---

### DevOps and Operations

**Site Reliability Engineering: How Google Runs Production Systems**
- **Focus:** SRE practices and principles
- **Key Topics:** Reliability, monitoring, incident response

**Проект Феникс про DevOps**
- **English:** The Phoenix Project (DevOps novel)
- **Focus:** DevOps transformation story

## Management Books

### Agile and Scrum

**Scrum и XP: заметки с передовой»** by Henrik Kniberg
- **English:** Scrum and XP from the Trenches
- **Focus:** Practical Scrum and XP implementation
- **Key Topics:** Real-world Agile practices

---

### Team Management

**«Человеческий фактор…»** by Tom DeMarco and Timothy Lister
- **English:** Peopleware: Productive Projects and Teams
- **Focus:** Human aspects of software development
- **Key Topics:** Team dynamics, productivity, management

**The Five Dysfunctions of a Team**
- **Rating:** 5/5
- **Focus:** About how a new CEO brought a team out of crisis
- **Key Insight:** The role of CEO is to create a team and maintain necessary interpersonal interaction between team members

**Influence Without Authority**
- **Focus:** Leading without formal authority
- **Key Topics:** Influence, persuasion, leadership

---

### Project Management

**Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK)**
- **Focus:** Comprehensive project management guide
- **Key Topics:** PMI standards and practices

**Tom DeMarco - Deadline**
- **Focus:** Project management novel
- **Key Topics:** Project delivery, team management

**Теория ограничений систем Голдратта**
- **English:** Theory of Constraints by Goldratt
- **Focus:** System optimization and bottleneck management

**Азиатская модель управления**
- **English:** Asian Management Model
- **Focus:** Alternative management approaches

---

## Best Practices

1. **Start with fundamentals** - Build strong foundation before advanced topics
2. **Mix theory and practice** - Combine books with hands-on projects
3. **Invest in education** - Structured courses complement self-study
4. **Read reviews** - Check ratings before investing time
5. **Apply learnings** - Practice concepts from books in real projects
6. **Share knowledge** - Post on LinkedIn after reading important books
