## Core Competencies

### Technical Leadership

- **Architectural Vision** - Ability to design and communicate system architecture
- **System Design** - Designing scalable and maintainable systems
- **Technical Breadth** - Broad understanding of technologies and tools
- **Documentation** - Creating and maintaining technical documentation
- **Code Architecture** - Designing clean, maintainable code structures
- **Tool Mastery** - Proficiency with development tools and platforms
- **Code Writing** - Ability to write high-quality code
- **Testing and Bug Fixing** - Testing strategies and debugging skills
- **Code Review** - Reviewing code and ensuring quality standards

---

### Process Management

- **Creating and Maintaining Processes** - Establishing and improving development processes
- **Team Management** - Leading and coordinating team efforts
- **Delegation** - Effectively assigning tasks and responsibilities
- **Conflict Resolution** - Handling team conflicts constructively

---

### Stakeholder Management

- **Stakeholder Identification** - Recognizing key stakeholders
- **Stakeholder Communication** - Effective communication with stakeholders
- **Requirements Gathering** - Collecting and understanding requirements
- **Business Understanding** - Understanding business context and strategic thinking
- **Critical Thinking** - Analytical and problem-solving skills

---

### People Development

- **Mentoring** - Developing team members' skills
- **Team Collaboration** - Fostering effective team collaboration
- **Cross-Team Interaction** - Working with other teams effectively

---

## Soft Skills Questions

### Conflict and Communication

1. **How do you handle conflict within your team?**
   - Look for: Constructive approach, mediation skills, focus on solutions

2. **How do you ensure effective communication within your team?**
   - Look for: Regular meetings, clear channels, documentation practices

3. **How do you handle underperforming team members?**
   - Look for: Supportive approach, clear feedback, development plans

---

### Process and Methodology

4. **What project management methodologies are you familiar with, and which do you prefer?**
   - Look for: Knowledge of Agile, Scrum, Kanban, etc., and reasoning for preference

5. **Can you discuss your experience with software development life cycles (SDLC)?**
   - Look for: Understanding of different SDLC models and their application

6. **How do you manage documentation?**
   - Look for: Documentation strategy, tools, and practices

---

### Personal Development and Experience

7. **Do you have any pet projects? What approach do you use?**
   - Look for: Initiative, learning mindset, practical application

8. **Do you have startup experience as a founder?**
   - Look for: Entrepreneurial mindset, risk-taking, business understanding

9. **Have you worked for a foreign company?**
   - Look for: International experience, cultural adaptability

---

### Tools and Resources

10. **What is your favorite task tracking tool?**
    - Look for: Tool knowledge, reasoning for preference

11. **What are your favorite books about management?**
    - Look for: Continuous learning, management philosophy

12. **Tell us about rituals in the team, meetings, 1-1, etc.?**
    - Look for: Team culture, communication practices, regular feedback

---

### People Management
- Team leadership and mentoring
- Conflict resolution
- Communication and collaboration

### Business Acumen
- Understanding business requirements
- Strategic thinking
- Stakeholder management

### Continuous Learning
- Pet projects and personal development
- Reading and knowledge sharing
- Adaptability to new technologies
