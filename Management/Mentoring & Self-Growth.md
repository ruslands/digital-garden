## Tips for Finding a Mentor

1. **Define your goals** - Know what you want to learn
2. **Be proactive** - Reach out to potential mentors
3. **Prepare questions** - Make the most of mentoring sessions
4. **Show commitment** - Demonstrate you're serious about growth
5. **Give back** - Consider becoming a mentor yourself
