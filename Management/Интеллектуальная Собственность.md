## Introduction

**РИД (Результат Интеллектуальной Деятельности)** - Result of Intellectual Activity

**Key Principle:** Results of intellectual activity are created by creative work and always have an author.

**Important:** Results of intellectual activity must be recognized as intellectual property and registered on the company's balance sheet.

## Trademark Registration Process

### Application Submission

**Notification of Application Acceptance (Form 940):**
- Document confirming the fact of submission and your priority right to the trademark (also called priority certificate)
- Often used as a title document, since the right to use a trademark arises from the moment of application
- **Formally**, a trademark can be used from the date of application, but **it's safer to wait for registration completion**

**Timeline:** Within 2 weeks, it is necessary to pay the fee (2 payments) according to these details (can be paid via QR code).

**Payment Method:**
- Preferably pay from personal card or personal/company account of the applicant
- Send payment documents (receipts, invoices, etc.) after payment

**Important Notes:**
- Do not pay attention to charges in Gosuslugi (if any)
- **If the fee is not paid within 2 months, Rospatent will increase it by 100%**

## Patent Types Comparison

### 1. Invention (Изобретение)

**Definition:** Изобретение – это новое техническое решение задачи, которое может относиться к устройству, способу, веществу или биологическому материалу.

**Patentability Criteria:**
- Novelty (новизна)
- Inventive step (изобретательский уровень)
- Industrial applicability (промышленная применимость)

**Protection Period:** 20 years from the date of application

**Rights:** Patent on invention provides exclusive right to its use. This is the most complete and strong protection.

**Application Area:** Usually for complex technical solutions with high level of innovation.

---

### 2. Utility Model (Полезная Модель)

**Definition:** Полезная модель – это техническое решение, относящееся преимущественно к устройствам.

**Patentability Criteria:**
- Novelty (новизна)
- Industrial applicability (промышленная применимость)
- **Note:** Inventive step is NOT required

**Protection Period:** 10 years from the date of application

**Rights:** Patent on utility model provides right to its use, however protection is weaker compared to invention.

**Application Area:** Usually for improvements in existing technical devices, when requirements for inventive step are not so high.

---

### 3. Industrial Design (Промышленный Образец)

**Definition:** Промышленный образец защищает внешний вид изделия, включая форму, конфигурацию, рисунок, окраску и прочие элементы дизайна.

**Patentability Criteria:**
- Novelty (новизна)
- Originality (оригинальность)

**Protection Period:** 5 years with possibility of extension up to 25 years

**Rights:** Patent on industrial design protects the appearance of the product from copying.

**Application Area:** Applied for design and external solutions, not for technical aspects.

---

### Summary Table

| Patent Type | Criteria | Protection Period | Strength |
|-------------|----------|------------------|----------|
| **Invention** | Novelty, Inventive Step, Industrial Applicability | 20 years | Strongest |
| **Utility Model** | Novelty, Industrial Applicability | 10 years | Moderate |
| **Industrial Design** | Novelty, Originality | 5 years (extendable to 25) | Design protection |

**Conclusion:** The choice between these three patent categories depends on the nature and purpose of the object that needs to be protected.

## Invention vs Trade Secret

### 1. Invention

**Definition:** Изобретение — это новое техническое решение задачи (устройство, способ, вещество и т.д.), которое может быть запатентовано.

**Protection:**
- To obtain a patent, must file an application and pass examination for novelty, inventive step, and industrial applicability
- Patent issued for limited period, usually 20 years

**Rights:** Patent provides exclusive right to use the invention and protects against unauthorized use by third parties.

**Limitations:** Patent content becomes public, and after the patent protection period expires, the invention passes into the public domain.

---

### 2. Trade Secret (Секрет Производства / Ноу-Хау)

**Definition:** Ноу-хау — это конфиденциальные знания и навыки (например, производственные процессы, рецептуры, методы), которые не патентуются, но имеют коммерческую ценность.

**Protection:**
- Protection of know-how is ensured by confidentiality measures
- May include non-disclosure agreements, access restrictions, and other security measures

**Rights:** Know-how does not require registration and can remain secret indefinitely, as long as confidentiality measures are maintained. Unlike a patent, which expires after a certain period, know-how can be kept secret forever.

**Limitations:** If know-how becomes known to a third party through legal means (e.g., reverse engineering), its protection is lost, and legal protection may be weaker than a patent.

**What Trade Secrets Protect:**
- Production information (Производственные сведения)
- Technical information (Технические сведения)
- Organizational information (Организационные сведения)

**Note:** Секреты производства (или «ноу-хау») обычно не подлежат публикации в государственных реестрах, так как их конфиденциальность является ключевым элементом их защиты.

---

### Key Differences

| Aspect | Invention (Patent) | Trade Secret (Know-How) |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------------|
| **Form of Protection** | Formal (patent) | Informal (confidentiality) |
| **Protection Period** | Limited (20 years) | Unlimited (if kept secret) |
| **Publicity** | Requires publication | Remains secret |
| **Loss of Rights** | Protected by law | Lost if disclosed legally |
| **Registration** | Required | Not required |

## Copyright vs Patent Protection

### Registration Requirements

**Require Registration (Patent Law):**
- Изобретения (Inventions)
- Полезные модели (Utility models)
- Промышленные образцы (Industrial designs)
- Товарные знаки (Trademarks)

- Бизнес-планы и стратегии (Business plans and strategies)
- Научные публикации (Scientific publications)
- Программные продукты (Software products)
- Видео и музыка (Video and music)

**Primary Protection:** Первичная охрана результатов творчества авторским правом (Primary protection of creative results by copyright)

## Author Rights

### Author Definition

**Key Principle:** Автор это всегда физическое лицо у него возникают права (Author is always a natural person, rights arise for them)

**For Inventions, Utility Models, Industrial Designs:**
- Автором изобретения, полезной модели или промышленного образца признается гражданин, творческим трудом которого создан соответствующий результат интеллектуальной деятельности
- Право авторства на служебное изобретение, служебную полезную модель или служебный промышленный образец принадлежит работнику (автору)

---

### Types of Author Rights

**Two Categories:**

1. **Неимущественные права (Non-property rights)**
   - Not transferable and not alienable
   - Always belong to the author
   - Cannot be transferred legally

2. **Имущественные права (Property rights)**
   - Exclusive rights
   - Can be transferred

---

### Working with Self-Employed (Самозанятые)

**Important:** При работе с самозанятыми важно знать, что авторские права делятся на две категории:

**Non-property Rights:**
- Право на авторство (Right to authorship)
- Право на имя (Right to name)
- **According to Civil Code:** Non-property rights always belong to the author. Transferring them legally is impossible. Even if the customer and author conclude a corresponding contract, from a legal point of view it will be invalid.

**Property Rights:**
- Everything related to using the result of intellectual activity and obtaining profit
- Can be transferred

**Example:**
- Company "Удобные диваны" orders a book about sofas from self-employed Ivanov
- Director Petrov publishes it under his own authorship
- **This is a violation of the law**

**Correct Approach:** Company can publish the book written by Ivanov under his authorship and receive profit from its distribution.

- When the entrepreneur acts as the sole author

- When third parties are involved in the work (e.g., employees)
- In such cases, need to conclude "contract for order with legal entity"

### Key Facts and Answers

**Most Valuable Assets:**
- **Answer:** Нематериальные активы (включая интеллектуальную собственность) (Intangible assets including intellectual property)

**Employer Rights on Service Works:**
- To preserve exclusive right on service work, employer must:
  - Начать использовать произведение (Start using the work)
  - Передать право другому лицу (Transfer right to another person)
  - Принять решение о сохранении произведения в тайне (Make decision to keep work secret)

**When Copyright Arises:**
- **Answer:** По факту создания результата интеллектуальной деятельности (Upon creation of result of intellectual activity)

**Initial Protection Mode:**
- **Answer:** Авторского права (Copyright)

**Protection Modes Available:**
- Авторского права (Copyright)
- Патентного права (Patent law)
- Как средство индивидуализации (As means of individualization)
- В режиме коммерческой тайны (In commercial secret mode)

**Valuation:**
- **Who Performs:** Оценщики и оценочные компании, являющиеся членами саморегулируемых организаций (Appraisers and appraisal companies that are members of self-regulatory organizations)

**Benefits of РЦИС.РФ Registration:**
- Надежность хранения информации (Reliability of information storage)
- Безвозмездность регистрации (Free registration)
- Отсутствие расходов на посредников и юристов (No expenses for intermediaries and lawyers)

**Management Accounting Allows:**
- О сроках правовой охраны (On terms of legal protection)
- Об авторах результатов интеллектуальной деятельности (On authors of intellectual activity results)
- Об использовании результатов интеллектуальной деятельности (On use of intellectual activity results)

**Costs May Include:**
- Затраты на оплату труда авторов (Costs for author compensation)
- Затраты на оплату услуг патентных поверенных (Costs for patent attorney services)
- Затраты на оплату государственных пошлин (Costs for state fees)

**Bank Financing Requirements:**
- **Answer:** Проведение независимой оценки стоимости прав (Conducting independent valuation of rights)

**Commercial Concession (Franchising):**
- **Best For:** Оказывающих масштабируемые услуги с использованием товарного знака (Companies providing scalable services using trademark)

**License Agreement Registration:**
- **Does NOT Require Registration:** Произведения (Works)

**Essential Condition of License Agreement:**
- **NOT Essential:** Срок использования результата интеллектуальной деятельности (Term of use of intellectual activity result)

**Cannot Use IP Rights For:**
- **Answer:** Для страхования рисков компании (For company risk insurance)

**Mandatory Feature of РИД:**
- **Answer:** Творческий характер и наличие автора (Creative nature and presence of author)

**Exclusive Right Requires State Registration For:**
- **Answer:** Изобретения (Inventions)

**Voluntary Copyright Registration Cannot Confirm:**
- **Answer:** Балансовую стоимость исключительного права на произведение (Balance sheet value of exclusive right to work)

**NOT Protected by Copyright:**
- **Answer:** Технические решения (Technical solutions)

**Trademark Allows Company To:**
- Предотвращать появление контрафактных товаров на рынке (Prevent appearance of counterfeit goods on market)
- Индивидуализировать товары и услуги компании для потребителя (Individualize company goods and services for consumer)
- Безопасно передавать права на логотип и символику компании контрагентам (Safely transfer rights to logo and company symbols to counterparties)

**Patent Protection NOT Suitable For:**
- **Answer:** Логотипов (Logos)

**For Patent Application Help:**
- **Answer:** К патентным поверенным (To patent attorneys)

**Patent Protection Period for Inventions in Russia:**
- **Answer:** 20 лет (20 years)

**Invention Formula Defines:**
- **Answer:** Объем правовой охраны изобретения (Scope of legal protection of invention)

**Patentability Criteria for Invention:**
- Новизна (Novelty)
- Изобретательский уровень (Inventive step)
- Промышленная применимость (Industrial applicability)

**For Foreign Patent Protection:**
- **Answer:** Подать заявку в зарубежное патентное ведомство или заявку по процедуре РСТ (File application in foreign patent office or application via PCT procedure)

**Registering РИД as Intangible Asset Allows:**
- Сформировать первоначальную стоимость активов (Form initial asset value)
- Повысить капитализацию компании (Increase company capitalization)
- Увеличить инвестиционную привлекательность компании (Increase company investment attractiveness)

**Exclusive Right on Service РИД:**
- **Answer:** Первоначально возникает у работника и принадлежит компании при соблюдении определенных условий (Initially arises with employee and belongs to company under certain conditions)

**Valuation Approach NOT Used:**
- **Answer:** Инвестиционный подход (Investment approach)

**Bank Financing Using IP:**
- **Answer:** Осуществляется залог исключительного права (Pledge of exclusive right is executed)

**Complex License Agreement:**
- **Answer:** Договор коммерческой концессии (франчайзинга) (Commercial concession (franchising) agreement)

**Exclusive License Prohibits Licensor:**
- **Answer:** Заключение иных лицензионных договоров и самостоятельное использование результата интеллектуальной деятельности (Concluding other license agreements and independent use of intellectual activity result)

**License Agreements Possible For:**
- Патентного права (Patent law)
- Авторского права (Copyright)
- Секрета производства (ноу-хау) (Trade secret (know-how))

## Summary

| Category | Key Points |
|----------|------------|
| **Trademark** | 10-year protection, can register as individual, fee payment within 2 weeks |
| **Patents** | Territorial, 3 types (Invention, Utility Model, Industrial Design) |
| **Copyright** | Arises automatically, can be registered voluntarily |
| **Trade Secrets** | Unlimited protection if kept secret |
| **Registration** | Required for patents/trademarks, voluntary for copyright |
| **Tax Benefits** | ПАК registration: 7.6% insurance, 0% profit tax |
| **International** | No international patents, must file per country, PCT extends to 2.5 years |
| **Management** | Can be pledged, shown to investors, put on balance sheet |

## Best Practices

1. **Register early** - File applications as soon as possible
2. **Pay fees on time** - Within 2 weeks to avoid 100% increase
3. **Document everything** - Keep all contracts, acts, and correspondence
4. **Monitor patent landscape** - Before launching new projects
5. **Use patent attorneys** - For patent purity and complex cases
6. **Put НМА on balance** - Increase company value
7. **Plan international filing** - Within 1 year or use PCT for 2.5 years
8. **Protect trade secrets** - Use NDAs and confidentiality measures
